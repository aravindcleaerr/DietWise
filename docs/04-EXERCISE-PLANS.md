# DietWise - Exercise Plans (12-Week Progressive Program)

## Important Disclaimer
Consult a physician before starting, especially for pre-existing conditions (heart disease, diabetes, hypertension, joint problems).

## Exercise Principles for 50+ Male
- **Start low, go slow** - Begin at comfortable intensity, progress gradually
- **Four pillars**: Aerobic, Strength, Flexibility, Balance
- **Talk test**: During moderate exercise, can talk but not sing
- **10% rule**: Don't increase volume more than 10% per week
- **Joint-friendly**: Low-impact exercises to reduce injury risk

## Weekly Minimums (CDC/ACSM for Adults 50+)
- 150-300 min moderate-intensity aerobic activity/week
- 2+ days muscle-strengthening targeting all major groups
- Balance training 3+ days/week

## Bodyweight Exercise Form Guide

### Chair Squats
1. Stand in front of sturdy chair, feet shoulder-width apart
2. Arms extended forward or crossed at chest
3. Push hips back, bend knees as if sitting
4. Lightly touch chair seat (don't plop down)
5. Drive through heels to stand
6. Keep knees tracking over toes

### Wall Push-ups
1. Stand arm's length from wall
2. Palms flat at shoulder height and width
3. Body in straight line (engage core)
4. Bend elbows to bring chest toward wall (45-degree angle)
5. Push back to start
6. Don't lock elbows at top

### Planks
1. Forearms and knees (modified) or forearms and toes (full)
2. Elbows directly under shoulders
3. Body in straight line - no hip sag or pike
4. Engage core (belly button to spine)
5. Breathe steadily (don't hold breath)
6. Look at floor (neutral neck)

### Standing Leg Raises
1. Stand next to wall/chair for balance
2. Standing leg slightly bent
3. Raise one leg forward to ~45 degrees
4. Hold 1-2 seconds at top
5. Lower slowly
6. Repeat both sides

### Glute Bridges
1. Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat
2. Lift hips until straight line from shoulders to knees
3. Hold 2-3 seconds at top
4. Lower slowly

## Warm-Up Routine (5-10 min - BEFORE every session)

Dynamic movements, 30-60 seconds each:
1. March in Place (60 sec)
2. Arm Circles (30 sec forward + 30 sec backward)
3. Leg Swings - Front/Back (10 per leg)
4. Leg Swings - Side to Side (10 per leg)
5. Hip Circles (10 per direction)
6. Torso Twists (10 per side)
7. Ankle Circles (10 per foot)
8. Bodyweight Half Squats (10 reps)

## Post-Workout Stretching (10-15 min - AFTER every session)

Hold each 20-30 seconds, don't bounce:
1. Standing Quad Stretch
2. Standing Hamstring Stretch
3. Standing Calf Stretch
4. Hip Flexor Stretch
5. Chest Doorway Stretch
6. Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch
7. Triceps Stretch
8. Cat-Cow Stretch
9. Seated Figure-4 Stretch
10. Neck Stretches

## Warning Signs - STOP Immediately

### Cardiac (Call Emergency)
- Chest pain/pressure/tightness
- Pain radiating to left arm, jaw, neck, back
- Sudden shortness of breath
- Heart palpitations or racing heart at rest
- Lightheaded, dizzy, or faint
- Cold sweats, nausea during moderate exercise

### Musculoskeletal (Stop, Rest, See Doctor)
- Sharp, sudden joint pain
- Popping/snapping in joint followed by pain
- Rapid joint swelling
- Numbness/tingling in extremities
- Back pain radiating down legs

### Heart Rate Guidelines (Age 50)
- Max HR: 220 - 50 = **170 BPM**
- Moderate Zone (50-70%): **85-119 BPM**
- Vigorous Zone (70-85%): **119-145 BPM**
- Do Not Exceed: **145 BPM** without medical clearance

## Progress Tracking Metrics

- Date, exercise type, duration, intensity (RPE)

- Total exercise minutes (goal: 200-300 min/week by Phase 3)
- Number of sessions (goal: 5-6/week)
- Body weight (same scale, same time, weekly)
- Waist circumference

### Monthly Benchmarks
- Walking distance in 15 min (should increase 5-10%)

- Resting heart rate (should decrease)

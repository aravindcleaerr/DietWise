# DietWise - Indian Vegetarian Diet Data

## Calorie & Macro Targets

### BMR Calculation (Mifflin-St Jeor)
- BMR = (10 x 94) + (6.25 x 170) - (5 x 50) - 5 = **1,748 kcal/day**
- TDEE (Lightly Active) = 1,748 x 1.375 = **~2,400 kcal/day**
- **Target intake: 1,500-1,800 kcal/day** (500-750 kcal deficit)

### Macro Split
| Macro | % of Calories | Grams (at 1,700 kcal) | Per Meal |
|-------|--------------|----------------------|----------|
| Protein | 25-30% | 106-128 g | 25-35 g |
| Carbohydrates | 40-45% | 170-191 g | 40-50 g |
| Fat | 25-30% | 47-57 g | 12-15 g |
| Fiber | -- | 30-38 g | 8-10 g |

### Key Micronutrient Targets
- Calcium: 1,000-1,200 mg/day (bone health at 50+)
- Vitamin D: 800-1,000 IU/day
- Vitamin B12: 2.4 mcg/day (critical for vegetarians)
- Iron: 8 mg/day
- Omega-3: 1.6 g/day (flaxseeds, walnuts)

## Portion Control Tips

### The Hand Method
- **Protein** (dal, paneer, curd): 1 palm-sized portion per meal
- **Carbs** (rice, roti): 1 cupped hand per meal (= 1 katori rice OR 2 phulkas)
- **Vegetables**: 2 fist-sized portions per meal (half the plate)
- **Fats** (ghee, oil, nuts): 1 thumb-sized portion per meal
- **Fruits**: 1 fist-sized portion per snack

### The Plate Method
- 50% = Vegetables and salad
- 25% = Protein-rich foods (dal, chole, paneer, sprouts)
- 25% = Complex carbs (roti, rice, millets)

### Key Rules
- Use a smaller thali (9-10 inch instead of 12 inch)
- Pre-serve portions on plate; don't eat from serving bowl
- Fix roti count before sitting (2 for lunch, 1.5-2 for dinner)
- Never more than 1 small katori rice per meal
- Eat slowly: 20 minutes per meal, chew 20-25 times
- Start with salad and soup first
- Drink water 30 min before meals
